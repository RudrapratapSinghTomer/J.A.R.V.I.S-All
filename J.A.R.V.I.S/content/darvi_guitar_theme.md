## 🎛️ Effects Chain

```
Guitar → Noise Gate → Compressor → Chorus → Delay → Reverb → Amp
                              ↓
                        (Bridge section: Harmonic Enabler)
```

### Recommended Settings:
- **Reverb:** Hall, 2.5s decay
- **Delay:** 450ms, 3 repeats, 30% mix
- **Chorus:** Rate 0.8Hz, Depth 45%
- **Compression:** Ratio 3:1, fast attack
- **Distortion (Chorus only):** Gain 60%, Tone 55%

## 🎯 Performance Notes

1. **Intro:** Play with fingers, not pick. Let each note ring fully.
2. **Verse:** Tight palm muting. Precision over speed.
3. **Chorus:** Switch to pick. Full distortion. Bend the 7th fret B string slightly.
4. **Bridge:** Light touch for harmonics. Position pickup selector to neck.
5. **Outro:** Return to fingerstyle. Fade volume knob gradually.
